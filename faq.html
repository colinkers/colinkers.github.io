FAQ

Your Questions. Answered.

Q 1. I only want to organise event for a small no. Of people. Can you still hep?

Ans. We can assist with an event of any size. Please call us to discuss your initial ideas and we can offer advice and prepare proposals tailored to your exact requirements.

Q 2. If I already have some ideas and have started planning, can you still help ?

Yes. We will combine your ideas with our wealth of operational experience and reliable business contacts to assist you with your event planning.

Q 3. How soon should I contact you before I want to plan an event?

You should contact us as soon as possible to ensure we have plenty of time to discuss your ideas and research the best options for you. The earlier we start to discuss your event the better opportunity we will have to secure the perfect venue, entertainment, suppliers, accommodation, flights and other elements within your budget.

Q 4. Do I need to attend any site visits / other meetings?

You can be as involved as you want to be! However it is not necessary for you to attend any meetings or site visits, as we can handle all of this for you – we are your safe pair of hands and we will be working on your behalf.

Q 5. What event services do you offer ?

Ans. We offer everything you need for your next event, including event design, event management, event marketing, event catering, event entertainment, and all the other myriad services that help make every event we plan a huge success.

Q 6. Do you charge separately for your various event services ?

Ans. Some of our services come standard, others are priced separately. In order to get an idea of what your event will cost, contact us to speak with one of our friendly event planners today. They can give you a free estimate based on your individual needs.

Q 7. Who handles my event?

Ans. We have a whole team of event planners, event designers, and event managers to meet your every need. They handle all the planning and arrangements. On the day of your event, they will be on hand to make sure everything runs smoothly so that you can focus on having a good time with your guests.

Q 8. Is Colinkers Event Management confined to a set region?

No, we are not limited to any set region as we are an online event management firm i.e. you can manage an event in Chandigarh while being based in Bomabay. All you require is a PC with an internet connection.

Q 9. What types of payment options are available with Colinkers ?

Colinkers has a wide array of payment options such as credit cards, debit cards, net banking, cash cards and mobile payments.
